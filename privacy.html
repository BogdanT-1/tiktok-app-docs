Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-28

What we collect

  • A TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token, stored locally on the user's machine in tiktok_tokens.json. Used only to upload videos to the authenticated TikTok account.
  • The video files the user produces, stored locally on the user's machine.

What we share

Nothing. Tokens never leave the user's machine. Videos are sent only to TikTok, at the user's explicit request.

Retention

Tokens are kept until the user deletes tiktok_tokens.json or revokes the application's access from TikTok account settings.

Third parties

The app calls the TikTok API on the user's behalf. No analytics, tracking, or third-party services are used.
