# Dub Translator

A small personal tool that downloads a TikTok video, generates dubbed (translated) versions of it using AI, and lets the operator upload the result as a **draft** to their own TikTok account.

The app is single-user, local-first, and does not store user-uploaded content beyond the duration of the requested job.
