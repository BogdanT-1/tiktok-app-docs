# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 3 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Dub Translator** ("the App", "we", "us") handles information about you when you use the App.

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Account information
When you sign up, our authentication provider (**Supabase**) stores:

- Your email address.
- A cryptographically hashed version of your password (we never see or store the plain-text password).

### 1.2 TikTok information (only if you publish to TikTok)
If you connect your TikTok account through the App, TikTok issues us an OAuth **access token** and **refresh token** scoped to:

- `user.info.basic` — your TikTok user ID and basic profile.
- `video.upload` — permission to upload video files as drafts to your TikTok inbox.

These tokens are stored locally on the machine running the App (`tiktok_tokens.json`). They are **not** transmitted to us or to any third party other than TikTok itself when refreshing the token or uploading a draft.

### 1.3 Content you submit
When you submit a TikTok video URL, the App temporarily downloads the video and its audio track to perform transcription, translation, voice synthesis, and re-encoding.

### 1.4 Technical data
We do not run our own analytics. Standard logs (IP, request timing) may be generated by Supabase or other hosting infrastructure used to run the App.

## 2. How we use information

We use the information described above only to:

- Authenticate you and gate the App behind a login.
- Generate the dubbed videos you request.
- Upload the resulting video as a **draft** to your TikTok account, when you click the publish button.

We do not sell, rent, or use your data for advertising.

## 3. Third parties that process your data

To provide the service, the App sends data to the following processors:

| Service | Data sent | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **TikTok** | OAuth tokens, video file, video metadata | Authentication and draft upload |
| **OpenAI** | Audio file, transcript text | Speech-to-text and translation |
| **ElevenLabs** | Audio sample, translated text | Voice cloning and speech synthesis |
| **Supabase** | Email, hashed password, session tokens | User authentication |

Each of these providers operates under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

- TikTok — [https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- OpenAI — [https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- ElevenLabs — [https://elevenlabs.io/privacy](https://elevenlabs.io/privacy)
- Supabase — [https://supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

## 4. Data retention

- **Source videos and intermediate audio files** are written to a temporary working directory on the host machine and deleted automatically when no longer needed for the current job.
- **Generated MP4 / SRT files** remain on the host machine (in the `output/` folder) until you delete them. They are not uploaded anywhere except TikTok, and only when you press the publish button.
- **Account credentials** persist in Supabase for as long as your account exists.
- **TikTok tokens** persist locally until you delete `tiktok_tokens.json` or revoke the App's access from your TikTok settings.

## 5. Your rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data tied to your account — request via the contact email below.
- **Delete** your account by emailing us; we will remove your record from Supabase.
- **Revoke TikTok access** at any time from your TikTok account settings → *Manage app permissions*. After revoking, the App can no longer upload drafts on your behalf even if `tiktok_tokens.json` still exists locally.

## 6. Security

We rely on industry-standard providers (Supabase, TikTok OAuth, HTTPS for all third-party calls). No system is perfectly secure; we cannot guarantee against unauthorized access, but we do not store sensitive payment data, plain-text passwords, or your TikTok content beyond what is needed for the active job.

## 7. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under that age.

## 8. International transfers

Because we use third-party services that operate globally, your data may be processed in countries other than your own, including the United States and the European Union. Each provider listed in Section 3 publishes its own data-transfer disclosures.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated through the App.

## 10. Contact
